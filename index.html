Fear Killer PvP Provisions

This document serves as the official codified PvP legal framework under the Fear Killer Rule Provisions.

Rules

  • No Bed Destruction - Violator's own bed will be removed.
  • No Fireball Usage - Banned for knockback abuse.
  • No Knockback Dory - Trident banned.
  • No Wind Charges
  • No Tactical Totems
  • No Potions or Potion Orbs
  • No Third-Party Interference
  • Rematch Protocol if agreed or ordered by ref.
  • Referee/Admin Oversight mandatory.
  • Allowed Items: Tracker Compass, Bridge Builder, Ender Pearls, Golden Apples.
  • No Spawn Killing
  • No Teaming in FFA
  • Health Reset Between Rounds
  • Match Confirmation & Countdown
  • Map Selection Clause
  • Gear Check by Referee

Penalties

  • 1st Offense: Verbal Warning
  • 2nd Offense: Removal from Server
  • 3rd Offense: PvP Suspension
