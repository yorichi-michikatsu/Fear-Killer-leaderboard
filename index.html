RP - Rank Points

Fire★Bolt Ranking System

  • Victory: +3 RP
  • Draw: +1 RP
  • Loss: 0 RP
  • Disqualification: -1 RP
  • Elite Duel Win: +2 RP
  • Voidblade Challenge (Partial): +6 RP
* Only official/refereed matches count toward RP.
Bronze (0–9 RP): Training & experimentation.
Silver (10–19 RP): Protocol basics, eligible for mentoring.
Gold (20–34 RP): Mid-tier. Tournament-eligible.
Platinum (35–49 RP): Advanced. May train others.
Blazing Core (50+ RP): Elite. May lead matches.
Solar Flame (75+ RP): Must defeat 3 Blazing Core.
Ember Warden (100+ RP): Trusted host/mod role.
Crimson Saint: Selected by leaders.
Voidblade Prime: 100+ RP & victory over 3 elites. Throne Challenge winner.

Fear Killer PvP Provisions

This document serves as the official codified PvP legal framework under the Fear Killer Rule Provisions.

Rules

  • No Bed Destruction - Violator's own bed will be removed.
  • No Fireball Usage - Banned for knockback abuse.
  • No Knockback Dory - Trident banned.
  • No Wind Charges
  • No Tactical Totems
  • No Potions or Potion Orbs
  • No Third-Party Interference
  • Rematch Protocol if agreed or ordered by ref.
  • Referee/Admin Oversight mandatory.
  • Allowed Items: Tracker Compass, Bridge Builder, Ender Pearls, Golden Apples.
  • No Spawn Killing
  • No Teaming in FFA
  • Health Reset Between Rounds
  • Match Confirmation & Countdown
  • Map Selection Clause
  • Gear Check by Referee

Penalties

  • 1st Offense: Verbal Warning
  • 2nd Offense: Removal from Server
  • 3rd Offense: PvP Suspension
