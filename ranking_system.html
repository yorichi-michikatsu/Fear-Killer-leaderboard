fire★bolt Ranking System

Official PvP Progression System of Fear†Killer (formerly cold†fire)

Purpose

To measure and reward PvP skill, consistency, and commitment through official Bedwars solo matches and custom duels.

RP (Rank Points) System

  • Victory (Official/Refereed Match): +3 RP
  • Draw (Tie): +1 RP each
  • Loss: 0 RP
  • Rule Violation / Disqualification: -1 RP
  • Elite Duel Victory: +2 RP per elite defeated (if not full Voidblade win)
  • Voidblade Challenge Attempt (Partial): +6 RP (if requirements not fully met)

Note: Only official matches and moderator/referee-watched duels count toward RP.

Ranking Tiers

1. Bronze (0–9 RP)
Beginner tier. No penalties for losing. Encouraged to experiment and train.
2. Silver (10–19 RP)
Developing fighters. Must follow basic duel protocols. Eligible for mentorship.
3. Gold (20–34 RP)
Recognized mid-level fighters. May be invited to participate in tournaments.
4. Platinum (35–49 RP)
Advanced PvP players. Trusted to help train newcomers.
5. Blazing Core (50+ RP)
Elite PvP warriors. Can lead/moderate matches. Targets for Solar Flame & Voidblade promotions.
6. Solar Flame (75+ RP)
Must defeat 3 Blazing Core in 1v1s. Gains access to elite training. PvP strategist.
7. Ember Warden (100+ RP)
Must have clean history. Can co-host camps & tournaments. PvP role model.
8. Crimson Saint (Selected Only)
No RP required. Chosen by leadership for loyalty, skill, or contribution.
9. Voidblade Prime (Only One Can Hold)
Must have 100+ RP. Defeat 3 elites (Solar/Ember/Crimson). Win Throne Challenge. Can issue Duel of Honor.
